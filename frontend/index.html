Tile Guide

Domain The start of your empire. Can claim adjacent tiles.
Mountain Impassable land. Cannot be claimed.
Forest Can claim adjacent tiles.
Plains Can claim tiles up to 2 spaces away.
Tower Can claim tiles exactly 3 spaces away.
Cave Can link to exactly one other cave on the map. Can claim adjacent tiles.
Wizard Can claim adjacent tiles. Provides a single-use action to claim any unclaimed tile.
Barbarian Raging warlords. When revealed, will unclaim all territories in their path.
